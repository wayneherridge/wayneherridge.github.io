Wedding Receptions

Catering

Whether you require a wedding buffet, formal dining or a barbeque, our chefs will provide wonderful food that is sure to impress your guests.

Menus can be chosen from our extensive range, which is regularly updated to reflect changing seasons and styles of presentation.

With our wedding package we will decorate your top table in a colour to match your theme and even provide bucks fizz with your sit-down meal.

Setup and Styling

Maybe you've made or sourced all of the styling details yourself and just need them setting up on your big day, or maybe you have a cool blank canvas venue and don’t know where to start. Perhaps the realisation has firmly set in that it's you and your best boys and best girls who will be the ones having to rush around decorating and styling in between hair and make-up – not exactly what you had in mind for the morning of your wedding day.

This is where I come in – I can work with you to brainstorm and moodboard ideas, suggesting suitable suppliers along the way or I can pull all your ideas together on the day and set up your venues as you have been planning for all these months!

Wedding Service

You've loved planning your wedding and everything about it, but want someone to manage your day and coordinate behind the scenes on the day itself to ensure that everything you've planned runs perfectly to plan.

My on-the-day wedding coordinator and management service lets you hand over the reins so you can concentrate on enjoying your day.

I'll go through your plans around one month before your day, contact all your suppliers to make any final arrangements, create a timeline that I will run on the day and will be on-site setting up the pretty and liaising with suppliers to make sure everything is running like a well-oiled wedding machine!
