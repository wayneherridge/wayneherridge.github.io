Proximity

Company Name

Newsroom of LDS.org

From this example we can see that LDS.org follows the rules of proximity by grouping related items toegther. The date and sub title of the webpage and also grouping the social media icons. This leaves the title, sub title and the paragraph underneath the social media icons to be the focal point of the page. The rules of proximity are evident throughout the site and help the site to look clean, uncluttered, and user friendly.
