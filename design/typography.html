Typography

Company Name

Audible - Audiobook Company

How Used

I received an email asking me to try Audible out and as I went exploring through the site I saw some great typography examples. The first thing you see on the home page is the white on black text. The text is arranged and formatted in such a way that is pleasing to the eye and made me want to explore more. The body of the site has a mixture of Arial and Sans Serif which keep the site easy to read and follow.
